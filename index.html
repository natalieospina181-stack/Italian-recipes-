Chapter 1 — Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g pancetta
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water.
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy.
  3. Mix eggs with grated parmesan.
  4. Drain pasta and mix with pancetta.
  5. Remove from heat and stir egg mixture quickly.
  6. Serve with extra cheese and pepper.
Chapter 2 — Traditional Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • 500g ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook beef with tomato sauce.
  2. Layer pasta sheets with meat and cheese.
  3. Repeat layers inside baking dish.
  4. Bake at 180°C for 40 minutes.
  5. Let rest before serving.
Chapter 3 — Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C.
  2. Roll dough onto pizza tray.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly.
  4. Add mozzarella slices.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes.
  6. Add basil and drizzle olive oil.
